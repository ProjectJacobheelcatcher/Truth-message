Genesis 1 – Who Created?

In Genesis 1, God is not YHWH (the Lord God) but "Elohim" (H430) — plural gods or angels with delegated power. The Lord God only appears in Genesis 2.

Genesis 1:1 — Listen
In the beginning God (Elohim) created the heaven and the earth.
Genesis 1:27
So God (Elohim) created man in his own image... male and female.

Genesis 2 – The Real Creator Appears

In Genesis 2, the LORD God (YHWH) forms Adam. He breathes a soul into man. This is the true beginning of God's personal work.

Genesis 2:2 — Listen
And on the seventh day God (Elohim) ended his work and rested.
Genesis 2:7
The LORD God formed man of the dust... and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life.

El H410 – The Name of Almighty God

Hebrew 'El' (H410) refers to Almighty God. Many biblical names reflect this — such as Emmanuel (God with us).

Isaiah 7:14 — Listen
A virgin shall conceive... and call his name Immanuel.
Matthew 1:23
They shall call his name Emmanuel, which means, God with us.
