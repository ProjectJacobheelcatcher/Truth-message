The Truth Warning Message

Part 1: Fundamentals and Scripture

The world is inverted. The devil lies and rules the earth’s systems. Christ alone is truth. Ask, seek, knock — and truth will be revealed.

Matthew 4:8–9
The devil offers Jesus all kingdoms of the world in exchange for worship.
John 8:43–45
Ye are of your father the devil... he is a liar, and the father of it.
Matthew 13:24–30
The wheat and tares grow together until harvest. Two spiritual races.
Ephesians 6:10–12
We wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against spiritual wickedness.

Part 2: Genesis and Elohim

Genesis 1 uses "Elohim" (plural). Genesis 2 introduces the Lord God (YHWH). This shows delegated authority, not direct creation by God Almighty.

Genesis 1:1 & 1:27
Elohim created the world and man in their image.
Genesis 2:2 & 2:7
The LORD God formed man and breathed life into him.
Isaiah 7:14 & Matthew 1:23
A virgin shall bear a son called Immanuel — God with us.

Part 3: Inversion, Sight, and the Twin System

Isaiah 29:15–16
Turning things upside down... the potter’s clay.
Isaiah 5:20–21
Woe to them that call evil good, and good evil.
Matthew 6:22–24
If your eye is single, your whole body will be full of light.
Psalm 35:19–20 / Proverbs 6:13
They wink with their eye — covert signals of evil.
2 Timothy 2:25–26
Recover yourself from the snare of the devil.
James 4:7–8
Submit to God... purify your hearts, ye double minded.

Part 4: You Are Gods, Light, and the Star

Psalm 82 / John 10:34
Ye are gods (Elohim), children of the Most High... Jesus confirms it.
Ezekiel 28:14–15
Lucifer, the cherub that covers, fell and covered us in flesh.
Ephesians 5:11–14
Reprove the unfruitful works of darkness. Awake, and Christ shall give thee light.
Matthew 2:2 / Numbers 24:17
The Star out of Jacob — prophecy of the coming King.

Part 5: Locusts, Birth by the Spirit, and Final Warning

Revelation 9:1–3
Locusts rise from the pit with power to torment.
John 3:3–5
Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.
Revelation 20:14–15
Whosoever not written in the book of life was cast into the lake of fire.
Joshua 24:15 / John 14:6
Choose this day whom you will serve. Jesus is the way, the truth, and the life.

Final Call

This is your wake-up call. Seek Jesus. Turn back to the Father. Ask for truth. Share the message while there's time.
