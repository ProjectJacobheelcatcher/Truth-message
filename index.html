The Truth Warning Message

Part 5: Locusts, Birth by the Spirit, and the Final Warning

The Locust Army and the Pit

In Revelation 9, a terrifying image appears: a bottomless pit opens, and locusts come forth with power to torment. These may symbolize transformed, fallen souls turned into agents of judgment.

Revelation 9:1–3 — Listen
And there came out of the smoke locusts upon the earth... and unto them was given power.

You Must Be Born Again

Jesus told Nicodemus: unless you are born again of water and of the Spirit, you cannot see or enter the Kingdom of God. Flesh birth is not enough.

John 3:3–5 — Listen
Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.

The Lake of Fire and the Second Death

There is a final destination for those who refuse truth and righteousness. Death and hell are cast into the lake of fire — this is the second death.

Revelation 20:14–15
And whosoever was not found written in the book of life was cast into the lake of fire.

Choose This Day Whom You Will Serve

God sets before us life and death, blessing and cursing — choose life. Jesus is the way, the truth, and the life.

Joshua 24:15
Choose you this day whom ye will serve.
John 14:6
I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me.

Final Call to Action

This message is a wake-up call to seek Jesus while He may be found. Read the scriptures. Cry out to the Father. Ask for truth. Share this page.

He who has ears, let him hear.
