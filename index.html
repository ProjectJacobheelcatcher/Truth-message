The Truth Warning Message

Part 1: Foundations of the Warning

Fundamentals

This world is inverted. People are so programmed from birth that most ignore the Bible fundamentals. The devil lies and runs the kingdoms of earth. God is our designated teacher. Pray for and desire Truth (Jesus is the Truth). Ask, Seek, Knock. Daniel 2 says that God is the revealer of secrets.

The Devil Runs the Kingdoms of Earth

The tenants of earth killed God's son and the prophets. They are a rebellious house and judgment is coming.

Matthew 4:8-9 — Listen
The devil sheweth him all the kingdoms of the world... "All these things will I give thee, if thou wilt fall down and worship me."

The Devil is a Liar

A crime syndicate led by the fallen angel Lucifer runs the kingdoms of earth. Jesus exposes him plainly.

John 8:43-45 — Listen
Ye are of your father the devil... he is a liar, and the father of it.

The Wheat and the Tares

Two kinds of people exist. The tares were sown among the wheat. We look alike but differ in spirit. You will know them by their fruit.

Matthew 13:24-30 — Listen
Let both grow together until the harvest...

Two Great Powers

Earth is between the Lord God above and the Devil below. We must put on the armor of God to stand.

Ephesians 6:10-12 — Listen
We wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against rulers of the darkness of this world.

Part 2: Genesis, Elohim, and Creation

Genesis 1 – Who Created?

In Genesis 1, God is not YHWH (the Lord God) but "Elohim" (H430) — plural gods or angels with delegated power. The Lord God only appears in Genesis 2.

Genesis 1:1 — Listen
In the beginning God (Elohim) created the heaven and the earth.
Genesis 1:27
So God (Elohim) created man in his own image... male and female.

Genesis 2 – The Real Creator Appears

In Genesis 2, the LORD God (YHWH) forms Adam. He breathes a soul into man. This is the true beginning of God's personal work.

Genesis 2:2 — Listen
And on the seventh day God (Elohim) ended his work and rested.
Genesis 2:7
The LORD God formed man of the dust... and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life.

El H410 – The Name of Almighty God

Hebrew 'El' (H410) refers to Almighty God. Many biblical names reflect this — such as Emmanuel (God with us).

Isaiah 7:14 — Listen
A virgin shall conceive... and call his name Immanuel.
Matthew 1:23
They shall call his name Emmanuel, which means, God with us.

Part 3: Inversion, The Eye, and Twin Spirits

Inversion: The World Turned Upside Down

The truth is hidden by inversion and lies. To truly see, you must flip the world upside down spiritually.

Isaiah 29:15-16 — Listen
Woe unto them that hide their counsel from the LORD... Surely your turning of things upside down shall be esteemed as the potter’s clay.

Liars: Calling Evil Good

The rulers who reject Christ invert good and evil. Their wisdom is prideful and deceitful.

Isaiah 5:20-21 — Listen
Woe unto them that call evil good, and good evil... Woe unto them that are wise in their own eyes.

The Eye: Light or Darkness

Your eye is the lamp of your body. A single eye brings light. A double mind brings darkness.

Matthew 6:22-24 — Listen
If thine eye be single, thy whole body shall be full of light... Ye cannot serve God and mammon.

Covert Signals: Winking & Deception

The enemy uses gestures and covert signals to signal deception and hatred without a cause.

Psalm 35:19-20 — Listen
Let not them that are my enemies wink with the eye that hate me without a cause.
Proverbs 6:13 — Listen
He winketh with his eyes... teacheth with his fingers.

Twin Spirits & Internal War

We are born into a system of duality — good vs evil — within ourselves. Only Christ can make us whole.

2 Timothy 2:25-26 — Listen
In meekness instructing those that oppose themselves... that they may recover themselves out of the snare of the devil.

Double Mindedness

Purify your heart. Draw near to God. Let Him make you single in purpose and Spirit.

James 4:7-8 — Listen
Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you... Purify your hearts, ye double minded.

Part 4: You Are Gods, Light, the Cherub, and the Star

Ye Are Gods – Elohim

Jesus quoted Psalm 82 saying we are gods (Elohim), children of the Most High. Yet we die like men. We are spiritual beings in flesh bodies.

Psalm 82:6 — Listen
I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High.

Jesus Quoting Psalm 82

Jesus defended Himself from accusations of blasphemy by referencing the law: “I said, Ye are gods.” The scripture cannot be broken.

John 10:30–35 — Listen
Is it not written in your law, I said, Ye are gods?

The Covering Cherub – Lucifer

Lucifer was the anointed cherub that covereth. After iniquity was found in him, he fell. He covered us with flesh. This is key to understanding the battle.

Ezekiel 28:14–15 — Listen
Thou wast upon the holy mountain of God... till iniquity was found in thee.

Reprove Darkness – Let the Light In

We are asleep — spiritually dead — until the light of Christ reproves and awakens us. The light makes all things manifest.

Ephesians 5:11–14 — Listen
Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light.

The Star and the Sign

The Bible links Jesus and prophecy with the star. There’s also the star from Jacob in Numbers. One eye to the Star — one eye to the Pit?

Matthew 2:2 — Listen
We have seen his star in the east, and are come to worship him.
Numbers 24:17
There shall come a Star out of Jacob, and a Sceptre shall rise out of Israel...
