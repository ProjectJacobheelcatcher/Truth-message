Part 4: You Are Gods, Light, the Cherub, and the Star

Ye Are Gods – Elohim

Jesus quoted Psalm 82 saying we are gods (Elohim), children of the Most High. Yet we die like men. We are spiritual beings in flesh bodies.

Psalm 82:6 — Listen
I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High.

Jesus Quoting Psalm 82

Jesus defended Himself from accusations of blasphemy by referencing the law: “I said, Ye are gods.” The scripture cannot be broken.

John 10:30–35 — Listen
Is it not written in your law, I said, Ye are gods?

The Covering Cherub – Lucifer

Lucifer was the anointed cherub that covereth. After iniquity was found in him, he fell. He covered us with flesh. This is key to understanding the battle.

Ezekiel 28:14–15 — Listen
Thou wast upon the holy mountain of God... till iniquity was found in thee.

Reprove Darkness – Let the Light In

We are asleep — spiritually dead — until the light of Christ reproves and awakens us. The light makes all things manifest.

Ephesians 5:11–14 — Listen
Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light.

The Star and the Sign

The Bible links Jesus and prophecy with the star. There’s also the star from Jacob in Numbers. One eye to the Star — one eye to the Pit?

Matthew 2:2 — Listen
We have seen his star in the east, and are come to worship him.
Numbers 24:17
There shall come a Star out of Jacob, and a Sceptre shall rise out of Israel...
